Facts About Polar Bears

Where are polar bears from?

Polar bears live in Artic regions like Canada, Alaska, Russia, Greenland and Norway.

Source: https://www.marinemammalcenter.org/animal-care/learn-about-marine-mammals/polar-bears#:~:text=Polar%20bears%20live%20in%20Arctic,%2C%20Russia%2C%20Greenland%20and%20Norway.

What do polar bears eat?

Polar bears eat Ringed seal, beared and other seals, carcasses, berries, seaweed and bigger pray alike.

Source: https://www.arcticwwf.org/wildlife/polar-bear/polar-bear-diet/

What do polar bears do to survive?

In the artic, temperature can drop to -40 degrees or even -46 degrees in winter and stay that way for days or weeks. But polar bears are built for those conditions from fur to skin,to paws and claws, the sea bear's physique is desgined to protect them from the cold, hunt seals, and dominate the Arctic.

Source: https://polarbearsinternational.org/polar-bears-changing-arctic/polar-bear-facts/adaptions-characteristics/
